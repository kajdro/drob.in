About Me

My name is Kaj Drobin and I'm a designer and co-founder of Tictail, the easiest, prettiest and most social way to sell stuff online. Before that I was Lead Digital Art Director and Partner at Identity Works, where I worked with clients like Spotify, Pocket Shop, Rodebjer and TV 4.

Prior to Tictail I co-founded Super Strikers, a digital agency and was Digital Creative Lead & Art Director at Identity Works. I'm passionate about creating meaningful digital experiences that combine aesthetics with functionality.

I live in Stockholm, Sweden, where I continue to explore the intersection of design, technology, and user experience.
